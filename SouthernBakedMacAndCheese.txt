Ingredients
===========
1 cup macaroni
2 cups sharp cheddar cheese grated
1 cup  parmesean cheese grated
3 cups milk
1/2 cup panko bread crumbs
2 tablespoons Smoked Paprika
2 tablespoons flour
2 1/2 tablespoons butter

Equipment
=========
Large caserol dish
Small saucepan
10" non-stick skillet

Instructions
============
1)  Prehead oven to 350 F.
2)  Cook macaroni per instuctions
3)  Over low-medium heat, melt butter.
4)  When butter is melted, stir in flour to make a roux.
5)  When roux is to your liking, stir the milk in slowly.
6)  Stir in sharp cheddar and parmesean cheese to start the cheese sauce.
7)  Stir cheese sauce occasionally until it is all melted and incorporated into the milk and thickened.
8)  When macaroni is done cooking, drain and put into the caserol dish.
9)  Brown bread crumbs over low-medium heat.  Set aside.
10) When cheese sauce is done, pour it over the macaroni that is in the caserol dish.
11) Add panko breadcrumbs to cheese sauce and macaroni. Stir.
12) Add paprika to mixture. Stir.
13) Put into the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
14) Take out of oven and serve immediately or let it cool off for 5 minutes.

Notes
=====
Goes well with hot wings and homefries.
